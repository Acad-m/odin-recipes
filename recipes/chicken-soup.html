Chicken soup

Description:

Fast and easy, yet delicious meal, for those cold wintery evenings

Ingredients:

  • 1 boneless chicken breast half, cooked and diced
  • 2 cups of water
  • 2 carrots, chopped to slices
  • 1 zucchini, diced
  • 1 clove of garlic, minced
  • Â½ teaspoon of chicken broth base

Steps:

  1. Put cooked chicken meat and water in a large pot and bring it to a boil.
  2. Add the carrots, zucchini, garlic and simmer all together for 5 - 10 minutes.
  3. Add the chicken broth and simmer for additional 5 minutes. Ready to serve.
